Privacy Policy

Welcome to the James Millikin Homestead website. We have created this Privacy Policy to outline our firm commitment to your privacy. Please read this policy to understand how the university treats any personal information you provide via this site. Note: This policy is subject to change.

Information Collection and Use

James Millikin Homestead is the sole owner of information collected via this website. The university does not sell, share, or rent such information in ways different from those outlined in this Privacy Policy.
Information about visitors to this site may be collected in the form of the following:

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on a user's hard drive that contains information about that user. Usage of a cookie does not link to any personally identifiable information. A user rejecting a cookie may still use this website. Cookies serve to track and target the interests of our users to improve this website.

Log Files

IP addresses also enable the university to analyze trends, administer this site, track users' movements, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Sharing

The James Millikin Homestead does not share aggregated demographic information with outside organizations or businesses.

Links

The James Millikin Homestead website contains links to other websites that are not affiliated with the university. The Homestead is not responsible for privacy practices of other sites; the Privacy Policy you are reading now applies only to this website. Some of the Homestead's business partners use cookies on our website (e.g. search services). The Homestead has no access to or control over these cookies. To learn more about the practices of our business partners, please view their Privacy Policies.

Security

The James Millikin Homestead takes every precaution to protect users' information. When a user voluntarily submits information via this website, that information is protected. All user information is restricted to use for the purpose(s) indicated to the user when that information is submitted. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific task are granted access to personally identifiable information. All employees are informed of the importance of, and any changes in, security and privacy practices as part of their training and in quarterly updates. Collected information is used for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy and thereafter filed in secure storage or discarded.

Guidelines for Children

Children under the age of 13 are asked not to submit any information via this site.
