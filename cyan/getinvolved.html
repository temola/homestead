The Homestead, Inc.

The Homestead, Inc., is a not for profit corporation created in 1979. The mission of the Homstead, Inc., is to restore and preserve the interior of this irreplaceable Decatur landmark, thereby raising community awareness of our historical heritage and promoting preservation for the future generations.

VAnother major purpose of the Homestead, Inc., is to oversee and help raise funding for major restorations on the homestead. Some previous restoration projects have included the parlor ceilings, dining room ceilings, and stained glass windows. The next restoration project is the ceiling frescoe in the master bedroom on the second floor.
