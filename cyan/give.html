Give to the Homestead

Giving to the homestead helps preserve and maintain this historic Decatur landmark. It helps the nonprofit Homestead Board continue the important work of historic community education done at the Homestead. Thank you for your support of this 1876 former home of a couple who had lasting positive impact on Decatur and the surrounding area!
